The following Rust crates That I have developped are openly published on crates.io.

lobatto v0.1.2

Gauss–Lobatto quadrature and interpolation points, spectral finite-element Lagrangian basis operations, with efficient hardcoded versions for low-order and low-dimension cases.

lobatto-fft v0.1.1

High-order FFT on Gauss–Lobatto grids and corresponding high-order solver for Poisson problems.

lobatto-spectral v0.1.0

High-order screened Poisson solvers on Gauss–Lobatto grids.
