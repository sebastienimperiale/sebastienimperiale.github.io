I am a Research Scientist (Chargé de Recherche, CRCN) in the AnankΞ team at CMAP, École polytechnique, Inria Saclay. I am also a Professeur Chargé de Cours (PCC, 64h) at CMAP, École polytechnique since November 2024.

My research focuses on the mathematical and numerical design of digital twin based upon PDE-based models, with emphasis on:

  • Wave propagation — asymptotic analysis of multi-scale and stiff wave problems, including elastic, electromagnetic, and hydro-acoustic waves
  • Robust numerical methods — spectral element methods (SEM), implicit-explicit (IMEX) schemes, local time-stepping, high-order time discretizations
  • Inverse problems & data assimilation — observer-based reconstruction, Kalman filtering, optimal control for medical imaging and non-destructive testing
  • Applications — cardiac elastography, corneal biomechanics, hemodynamics, ultrasonic NDT, ocean wave modelling
